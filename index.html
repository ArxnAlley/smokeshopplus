Why Smoke Shop+

Smoke Shop+ isn't just another smoke store — it's a curated experience. We blend premium smoke culture with a futuristic atmosphere built for enthusiasts who appreciate quality, service, and next-level design.

Every product on our shelves is selected with intention. From hand-blown glass to cutting-edge vape tech, we focus on quality gear, fair pricing, and an environment that feels modern, welcoming, and elevated.

Whether you're a daily regular or stepping in for the first time, Smoke Shop+ is built to feel different — cleaner, sharper, and more intentional than the average shop.

Built By Passion. Backed By Community .

Smoke Shop+ was founded with one simple goal: bring big-city quality and experience to Catlettsburg.

The vision behind the shop is rooted in community, authenticity, and raising the standard for what a local smoke shop can look and feel like.

This isn't just a storefront — it's a place built for culture, connection, and consistency.
